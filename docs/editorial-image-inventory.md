## Notes

- SVG assets are intentionally lightweight and explanatory. They avoid tiny unreadable text and use descriptive names.
- Existing product captures are reused where they show the real LiftPDF interface.
- Main article images are loaded near the top of each article. Secondary figures are lazy-loaded by default through Next/Image behavior.
- OpenGraph images remain route-specific where they already exist for tool clusters. Foundation guides use their guide image until dedicated OG images are added.
